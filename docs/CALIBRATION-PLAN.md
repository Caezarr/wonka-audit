# Score Calibration Plan

## Objective

Determine whether the directional components measure consistent practice quality without systematically rewarding a particular tool, role or activity volume.

## Dataset

- consented, minimized session features only;
- stratified sampling across tools, roles and seniority;
- preregistered exclusion rules;
- separate development and holdout cohorts;
- target at least 300 participants before external benchmark claims.

## Annotation

Three independent reviewers score context, workflow maturity and verification evidence using a written rubric. Report Krippendorff’s alpha or an equivalent agreement statistic. Resolve rubric ambiguity before changing score thresholds.

## Analyses

- test-retest stability on comparable windows;
- sensitivity to missing source capabilities;
- differential score behavior by tool and role;
- correlation with independently reviewed work quality;
- confidence intervals and minimum sample requirements;
- threshold calibration on the holdout cohort.

## Release gate

Do not rename the score “validated” or publish sector benchmarks until the methodology, sample sizes, uncertainty, bias analysis and negative findings are public and reproducible.
